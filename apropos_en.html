About Us

Aliance is an agency that coordinates and oversees various strategic projects: NGOs, blogs, for-profit organizations, and much more. We began in 2023, with a clear vision: to create sustainable and positive impacts through every initiative. Officially, we will operate starting December 30, 2024, with the ambition of building innovative solutions that transform local and global communities.
